Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my resume?

Simply click the 'Upload Resume' button from the dashboard, select your file, and our system will analyze your resume to help match you with relevant job listings.

How accurate are the job recommendations?

Our AI uses advanced machine learning models to match your profile with jobs. The more you interact with the platform, the more accurate the recommendations will become.

Can I save jobs for later?

Yes, you can save jobs and apply at a later time. Just click the 'Save' button on the job listing page.

How can I track my job applications?

You can track your job applications through your dashboard, which will show the status and updates of all the jobs you've applied to.
